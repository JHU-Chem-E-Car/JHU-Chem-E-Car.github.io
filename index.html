Johns Hopkins University ChemE-Car

Applying Engineering Skills to Reality.

Brief Summary

The Chem-E-Car team focuses on designing and building a fully chemically controlled car. The interdepartmental team brings students of different backgrounds in mechanical, chemical and electrical engineering as well as computer science together to achieve a unifying goal. As a part of the American Institute of Chemical Engineers, team members get an opportunity to compete against other universities at the regional level and then national level at the annual AIChe Conferences that take place all over the nation. Join Chem-E-Car to become part of a dynamic team of engineers solving real world challenges!
